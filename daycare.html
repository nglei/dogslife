Day Care Services













Is your pup stressed out from being left at home alone all day?
Maybe bored enough to eat the couch, your favorite shoes or the dining room table?

If you are having troubles about your favourite shoes and couch, we may help you to deal with your little troublemaker.




































Whole Day Daycare








Your dog will spend their day playing, swimming, exploring and resting with their friends at the Day Care Centre.

The centre is split up into separate fields to ensure there are never too many dogs in one space, with plenty of constant supervision, love and attention.

There are heated buildings with dog beds and outdoor gazebos for shade, so the dogs can have a snooze when tired or race around to their hearts content.









































Half-day Daycare *








Your dog will spend an action packed 2-3 hours at the Day Care Centre. This gives your dog a chance to enjoy the facilities, socialisation and stimulation at the Day Care Centre, without the expense of full day care.

When the dogs dropped home after lunch they are happily tired, and will sleep all afternoon until you return. We can keep a set of your keys, so you do not need to be home during this time.
